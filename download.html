How to Install on iPhone / iPad
1

Make sure you are using Safari (not Chrome or another browser).

2

Tap the Share button at the bottom of the Safari screen.

3

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen"

4

Tap Add in the top-right corner. The RHC Guide icon will appear on your home screen.

Add to Home Screen
Open your browser menu and tap "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen" to install the Rafiq-e-Hujjaj Guide on your device — no app store required.
